Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What Flew Over is operated by What Flew Over LLC. We take your privacy seriously.

What we collect

  • Email address — to notify you when we launch in your area
  • Zip code — to determine your location for personalized flight data

We do not collect payment information, precise location, or any behavioral data.

How we use it

Your email is used only to notify you when What Flew Over launches in your area, and subsequently to send you the daily digest if you choose to subscribe. We do not use it for advertising or share it with third parties.

Who we share it with

Nobody. We use the following services to operate the product:

  • Amazon Web Services (SES) — email delivery
  • Supabase — database (hosted in the US)
  • Railway — backend hosting

Removal

Email digest@greatplainsplanes.com at any time to be removed from the waitlist.
